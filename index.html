About StartUpConclave 2018


In order to contribute to the growing startup ecosystem amongst students, we are proposing a National level event Startup Conclave 2.0. In the present era, having business knowledge is a must. It has penetrated every field of our existence .The event aims at grooming the entrepreneurial aspect of the individual in the form of business & technology and implementing them in the real-life scenario. In addition to keynote, various sessions and events will be conducted during the program. Such interactions will facilitate a better understanding about start-ups amongst the peers and nurture the talent of young aspiring entrepreneurs.
